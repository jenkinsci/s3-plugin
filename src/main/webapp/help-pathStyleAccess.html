Enable Path-Style Access to S3.

The difference is as follows (assuming bucket name of "johnsmith.net"):

  • Host-Style Access: https://johnsmith.net.s3.amazonaws.com/homepage.html
  • Path-Style Access: https://s3.amazonaws.com/johnsmith.net/homepage.html
Some on-premesis S3 systems may require path-style access due to certificate handling, or network policies.
